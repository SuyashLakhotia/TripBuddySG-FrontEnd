Our Mission, Vision & Core Values

Mission Statement

To provide an effective medium through which individuals from different cultural backgrounds can form lifelong friendships through authentic, local experiences.

Company Vision

To be the brand every incoming exchange student in Singapore knows and acknowledges as the one-stop website to catalyze friendships and truly gain the Singapore experience.

Core Values

We believe in providing excellent customer support and maximising customer satisfaction for all services rendered.

We believe in focusing on the human element of the trips hosted on our platform rather than just the numbers involved.
