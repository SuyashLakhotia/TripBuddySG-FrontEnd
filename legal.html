Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: 21st December 2015

Welcome to the website (the "Site") of TripBuddy.SG ("TripBuddy.SG", "we", "us" and/or "our"). This Site has been created to provide a marketplace for local experiences (the "Services") for our users ("you", "your").

We take your privacy very seriously. We ask that you read this privacy policy (the "Privacy Policy") carefully as it contains important information about what to expect when we collect personal information about you ("Personal Data") and how we will use your Personal Data.

This Policy applies to information we collect on www.tripbuddy.sg and its sub-domains about:
i) visitors to the Site; and
ii) people who register accounts with us; and
iii) people who register their email addresses with us.

1. INFORMATION COLLECTED FROM VISITORS TO OUR WEBSITE

We will obtain Personal Data about you when you visit us or when you voluntarily provide such information such as when you register for access to the Services, use certain Services, contact us with inquiries or respond to one of our surveys. For example, we collect contact information, location, payment information, and other information that you provide to us. Wherever we collect Personal Data, we make an effort to provide a link to this Privacy Policy.

When you visit us, we may monitor the use of this Site through the use of cookies and similar tracking devices. For example, we may monitor the number of times you visit our Site or which pages you go to. This information helps us to build a profile of our users. Some of this data will be aggregated or statistical, which means that we will not be able to identify you individually. Please see further the section on "Our Use of Cookies" below.

We do not knowingly collect Personal Data from children under the age of 18. If you are under the age of 18, please do not submit any Personal Data through the Site. We encourage parents and legal guardians to monitor their children's Internet usage and to help enforce our Privacy Policy by instructing their children never to provide Personal Data on this Site without their permission. If you have reason to believe that a child under the age of 18 has provided Personal Data to us through this Site, please contact us, and we will endeavour to delete that information from our databases.

By providing us with Personal Data, you are consenting to our use of it in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you provide Personal Data, you acknowledge and agree that such Personal Data may be transferred from your current location to the offices and servers of TripBuddy.SG and authorised third parties.

2. INFORMATION REGARDING USERS WHO REGISTER THEIR EMAILS WITH US

When you register your emails with us, we may use it for the following:
a) We will send you weekly newsletters.
b) We will contact you about products and services we think may be of interest to you.
c) We will send you notification emails regarding signing up, registration and Services that you have purchased through us.

If you would rather not receive these emails, please send an email message to contact@tripbuddy.sg requesting to opt out.

By submitting your email you consent to the use of that email as set out in this policy.

3. OUR USE OF COOKIES

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behaviour information. The information is used to track visitor use of the Site and to compile statistical reports on Site activity. For further information about cookies visit www.aboutcookies.org or www.allaboutcookies.org. You can set your browser not to accept cookies and the above websites tell you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases some of our Site features may not function if you remove cookies from your browser.

4. HOW WE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION

We are committed to maintaining the security of personal information. We have put in place appropriate security procedures and technical and organisational measures to safeguard your personal information from loss, misuse, and unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. The security procedures include, but are not limited to, firewalls, basic encryption, limited access & use of passwords. However, no Internet, email or other electronic transmission is ever fully secure or error-free, so you should take special care in deciding what information you send to us in this way.

Subject to the exceptions referred to in section 21(2) of the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (No. 26 of 2012) of Singapore ("PDPA"), you have the right to request a copy of the information that we hold about you. If you would like a copy of some or all of your personal information, please send an email to contact@tripbuddy.sg or send a letter to Benjamin Ho at Blk 5 #08-01 Tanah Merah, Kechil Road, Singapore 466665. Fee & Booking Policy

LAST UPDATED: 21st December 2015

Your plans while you're on exchange in Singapore are always subject to change. Taking this into consideration, all local hosts on our platform commit to help you with a flexible booking policy. Below are our marketplace policies relating to fees, cancellations and refunds. It is at our sole discretion to amend these policies from time to time and it is your responsibility to regularly check the Site to determine if there have been changes.

1. FEES

There are two types of fees involved in our marketplace transactions. You may refer below for the detailed description of each fee. If you have any questions on our fee policy, please contact us at contact@tripbuddy.sg.

a. Processing Fee
For every booking between buyers and sellers, TripBuddy.sg collects a processing fee of 10% of the total value. This processing fee shall be deducted directly from the amount paid to us, and is non-refundable in all circumstances, unless your trip/activity/event is cancelled by the seller. The processing fee is used for our marketplace maintenance and geographical expansion to serve you better.

b. Transaction Fee
Our payment system accepts PayPal and credit/debit cards supported by PayPal. All payments transacted through PayPal are subject to a PayPal fee. The PayPal fee is comprised of a fixed component and a variable component. This fee is borne by sellers and according to PayPal, the variable component will be refunded on a pro-rata basis in case of approved cancellation. The PayPal fee and its refund policy may change from time to time at the discretion of PayPal. If you would like to know more about PayPal fees, please refer to the PayPal website.

2. CANCELLATION POLICY

You are allowed to request for cancellation of your booking(s) at least 24 hours prior to 12:00 AM local time of the activity date. All cancellation requests shall be reviewed by our team and the purchaser will be informed of the outcome by email. An approved cancellation request shall incur a processing fee of 10% of the total booking value. Late cancellation requests are considered a "no-show" and will incur full booking charges.

3. REFUND POLICY

Refunds arising from any cancellation shall be informed by messaging the seller & purchasers directly on their provided email addresses. Fund transfer will be processed within ten (10) working days from the email date.

If you have any questions about our policies, you can email us at contact@tripbuddy.sg.
